Analysis of Suitable Elephant Habitat, Narok and Kajiado Districts, Kenya

A series of maps modeling suitable elephant habitat in the Narok and Kajiado districts of Kenya, with recommended areas for further study where new protected areas could potentially be established. The analysis considers the location of existing protected areas, distribution of rainfall, locations of reported human elephant conflicts, travel distance from the Mara river, and areas of suitable elephant habitat to recommend locations for further study as potential new protected areas.
