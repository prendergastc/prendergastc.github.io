StoryMap: Harmony Headlands

In a secluded and peaceful setting along Highway 1, Harmony Headlands State Park adorns the stunningly beautiful Central Coast in San Luis Obispo County. The 784-acre coastal park is just 3 miles south of the tiny town of Harmony, offering artist galleries and a creamery. The Harmony Headlands State Park offers a pleasant 4.3-mile out-and-back trail.

This StoryMap combines maps, text, images, and video to present a short walk along the California coast at the Harmony Headlands.
